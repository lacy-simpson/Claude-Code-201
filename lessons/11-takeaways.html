Key Takeaways

If you remember only a handful of things from this course, make it these. They are the habits that separate a frustrating Claude Code session from a productive one.

Five habits

The Habits That Matter

  • One folder, one purposeStart sessions from a focused folder so Claude sees what the task needs and builds useful memory, not your whole machine.
  • One session per taskGive each task its own session to keep the context window clean and avoid cross-contamination and early compaction.
  • Pick the right model and effortStart light, move up on purpose, and glance at your usage so a heavy task does not surprise you.
  • Stay in charge of permissionsChoose the permission mode that fits the work, always-allow the safe reads, and read what you approve.
  • Own the outputClaude assists; you decide. Review the result before you use it, every time.
